Who We Are

Deriapur's Multi-Service Hub

Deep Studio was founded with a single mission: to provide the local community of Deriapur and nearby villages in West Bengal with top-tier services that are usually hard to access in rural zones.

We started as a modest photocopy and documentation kiosk, helping students and professionals get clean photostats and printouts. Sensing the local demand for specialized wedding and birthday photographers, we expanded into creative digital imaging.

To round out our community services, we added high-reliability transportation. Our Mahindra Bolero car rental service is now the most trusted choice in Deriapur for outstation tours, emergency travels, and wedding commutes.
