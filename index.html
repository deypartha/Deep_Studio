Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our studio photography, photostats, or Bolero renting? Find quick answers below.

What kind of events does Deep Studio cover?

We provide full-service professional photography and videography for marriages, anniversary celebrations, birthdays, rice ceremonies, and other religious and cultural festivals across Deriapur and surrounding districts in West Bengal.

Do you offer bulk document photostating/copying?

Yes! We have high-capacity duplex Xerox machines capable of handling hundreds of copies in black-and-white or color. We serve students, local offices, schools, and individuals with fast turnaround times and bulk discounts.

How can I rent a Bolero car and what are the rates?

Renting is simple. You can call us, send a WhatsApp message, or use our contact form. Our rates vary depending on duration (hourly/daily) and distance (local within Deriapur vs outstation trips). All bookings come with an experienced driver.

Can I customize my photography and car rental package?

Absolutely! We offer combined packages for marriages and birthdays. For example, you can book us for your wedding photography and hire our Bolero for wedding party transportation at a discounted combo rate.
